# What We Do

Birdseye perspective. Feature-level details live in [offer/what-clients-get.md](../offer/what-clients-get.md).

## The one-sentence version

CryptoGameplan (CGP) helps experienced crypto investors run their portfolios **systematically** — following where capital is actually flowing in the market, instead of guessing based on a personal thesis.

## The problem we solve

Most crypto investors don't have a system. They have a thesis — a bet on what will work, usually based on something they read, a coin they like, or a narrative that sounds right.

A thesis falls apart the moment the market stops agreeing with it. The investor then either holds through drawdowns they can't stomach, or panics and exits at the wrong time. The cycle repeats.

We replace the thesis with a process: rules-based positioning that responds to what the market is actually doing.

## Who it's for

Experienced investors who:
- Have been through at least one crypto cycle (ideally more)
- Already have meaningful capital in the market
- Are tired of operating on opinion and want structure
- Understand that drawdowns are real and want a system to handle them, not another prediction

## Who it's not for

- Beginners looking to learn crypto from scratch
- People chasing the next 100x
- Anyone who wants signals to copy without understanding why

## The handoff principle

Every lead the AI qualifies should understand, before the call: CGP replaces guesswork with a process, for investors who already have skin in the game and want to stop running their portfolio on instinct.
