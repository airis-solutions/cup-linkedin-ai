# Business — Agency Handoff Pack

Everything an external team (or an AI agent they build) needs to represent the business on LinkedIn: what we do, who we sell to, how the sales process works, how messages should sound, and where to send qualified leads.

**Built to be evergreen.** No numbers or facts that shift week-to-week. If something here goes stale, that's a bug — flag it.

---

## How to use this folder

- **Starting from zero?** Read [general-info/](general-info/) first, then [offer/](offer/).
- **Building outreach logic?** [sales-process/](sales-process/) and [dm-flows/](dm-flows/).
- **Writing copy?** [personal-voice/](personal-voice/) is the guardrail set.
- **Need to send a lead somewhere?** [links.md](links.md).

---

## What's in each folder

### [general-info/](general-info/)
- `what-we-do.md` — plain-language description of the business
- `icp.md` — ideal customer profile for LinkedIn targeting
- `founder-bio.md` — who Robin is (the AI speaks from a personal account)

### [offer/](offer/)
- `what-clients-get.md` — deliverables and structure
- `how-it-works.md` — the system at a conceptual level

### [sales/](sales/)
- `funnel-stages.md` — end-to-end journey: lead → qualification → call → close
- `objection-bank.md` — DM-stage objections and how the AI handles each one
- `handoff-protocol.md` — what happens when the AI successfully qualifies a lead
- `objections/` — deeper psychology reference for Robin's call prep (not the AI)

### [dm-flows/](dm-flows/)
- `qualification-flow.md` — the primary qualification script, AI-ready
- `opener-patterns.md` — how first-touch messages are written
- `follow-up-patterns.md` — cadence, nudges, when to park

### [personal-voice/](personal-voice/)
- `tone.md` — how messages should sound
- `banned-phrases.md` — words and claims that are off-limits
- `compliance-guardrails.md` — finance/crypto regulatory "never say" list

### [links.md](links.md)
All URLs the AI may need (booking page, website, socials).
