### **CGP\_Core\_Objections\_and\_Psychology**

**1\. The "Polite Lies" (What they say on the call)** When prospects want to exit a sales conversation without confrontation, they use these surface-level excuses. Our marketing must dismantle these before the call happens:

* *"I need to talk to my spouse/partner."*  
* *"Let me think about it and get back to you."*  
* *"I don't have the liquidity right now. I need to make back some of my recent losses first before I invest in a program."*  
* *"Now isn't the right time. Let's touch base in a few months."*

**2\. The "Ugly Truths" (What they actually feel)** These are the deep-seated fears of the "burned investor." They rarely admit these out loud:

* **The Bitcoin Rationalization:** "Altcoins are a scam/too hard. I'll just hold Bitcoin and do nothing." (Ignoring that BTC alone won't get them to their financial goals).  
* **Self-Doubt:** They don't trust *themselves* to actually execute the system. They fear they aren't smart enough or disciplined enough.  
* **The "Moon-Boy" Hangover:** They are secretly disappointed that our system isn't a magical 100x overnight fix. A legitimate, mathematical system feels "boring" compared to the hype they are used to.  
* **Paralysis:** The pain of staying in their current losing situation feels safer than the risk of making another $10,000 mistake.

**3\. The Friction Points (Where the deal breaks)**

* **The Price Shock:** They expect a cheap $3k-$5k "course," but we are pitching a premium $10,000 system/consulting offer.  
* **The Time Commitment:** They want a "done-for-you" push-button solution and get nervous when they realize building a real system takes effort and time.

**4\. The Alternative (What they do if they don't buy)** They do not go to competitors. They simply freeze. They hold their "dead bags" (underperforming tokens), continue using their broken trading strategies, and bleed money slowly while hoping the market magically saves them.

**5\. The "Comeback" Trigger** Lost deals frequently return 3 to 6 months later, admitting "You were right." This is triggered by consistent follow-up via "Platinum Updates" (high-value, transparent market breakdowns) that prove our system works while their "hold and wait" strategy fails.
